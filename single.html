The 7 AM Ritual: Prepping the Canvas

Before a single drop of foundation touches the skin, the canvas must be prepared. At MK GLAMZ, we believe that makeup is not a mask to cover, but a refinement of the individual's natural architecture. A flawless, long-lasting look relies 80% on preparation and 20% on application.

Step 1: Clarify & Hydrate

We begin with a gentle, non-stripping double cleanse to sweep away overnight residue. Following this, a lightweight hydrating essence is pressed gently into the skin. This swells the skin cells with moisture, softening fine lines and preparing the surface to absorb subsequent products.

Step 2: Massage & Lymphatic Drainage

Using light pressure, our artists perform a facial massage from the center of the face outwards. This stimulates circulation, promoting a natural blush and draining excess fluid. The result is a more sculpted cheek and jawline before any contour is applied.

For the final step, a botanical facial oil is warmed between the palms and patted onto the high points of the face. This creates a dewy, luminous grip that allows makeup to blend seamlessly.
